📘 Why does this site exist?

This site was created following a rental dispute resulting in a civil judgment (May 26, 2025, case 25A782/1, rejection of counterclaims and confirmation of €2,173.86 indexation) and a criminal complaint filed with the Leuven prosecutor's office (May 19, 2025), with civil party constitution.

Its purpose is exclusively documentary and legal. It makes available the documents, filings, and events related to this case in a neutral context, compliant with GDPR (Article 6.1.e and f), without comment, qualification, or interpretation.

The site's content is limited to documents officially produced or submitted to Belgian courts. No public dissemination or promotion has occurred. No statement is directed against the landlord personally outside of actual judicial content.

The site is not intended to damage anyone's reputation. It documents an unresolved legal conflict on merits and allows everyone to independently review the facts.
